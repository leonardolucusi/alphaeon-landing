Transforming the Connection Between Supermarkets and Consumers

The Challenge

Supermarket chains need to integrate their internal systems and deliver a modern, reliable, and personalized digital experience to their customers. The lack of real-time connectivity between ERPs and mobile devices limits operational efficiency and reduces consumer engagement.

Our Solution

We developed an intelligent integration platform that connects ERPs and mobile applications, seamlessly linking the entire data chain — from internal systems to the end consumer — with security, scalability, and high performance.

Key Differentiators

  • Integration with multiple existing ERPs
  • Real-time synchronization of inventory, prices, and promotions
  • Personalized discount notifications based on customer profiles
  • Centralized dashboard for real-time management
  • Fault-resilient architecture with guaranteed data integrity
  • Comprehensive observability system for all operations
  • End-to-end support and visibility across the supermarket network
